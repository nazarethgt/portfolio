Nazareth González

I am Nazareth a developer, creator and problem solver with an excess of curiosity and a lots of common sense. I've been developing things since the day I first made a design plan. Logical thinking and technology has always fascinated me and I've never been afraid to explore new tools, be it Paint, Canvas, Autocad or CSS. Throughout my life I have experimented in various professional areas, from the development of structural calculations and budgets to the execution of search and performance campaigns where marketing led me to something more and here I am developing web applications The thing that excites me the most about working in software development is being able to design and create things that have a purpose and solve real problems. Leaning on customer research and knowledge, finding the right problems to solve, delivering that value as quickly as possible, learning from it and then iterating and improving that value over time is the key to great design. One of my favorite quotes by Peter Drucker: “There is nothing so useless as doing efficiently what should not be done at all." This resonates so deeply with me because I have no desire to do things just because. I just want to make, design or build ideas and products that matter and have a positive impact on people's lives.
